Welcome To Yihone Chu's Star Viewing Guide

In 2017 I went on a twelve day back-packing trip to Philmont Scout Ranch with some of my best friends.
I remember stars filling up the entire sky and being completely overwhelmed.
Here in Ohio the stars are not as good as they are in New Mexico.
I have had to deploy methods and equipment to view stars more clearly.
This guide is here to help you view stars.

Things that you will need:

  1. High Power Telescope
  2. Telescope Tripod
  3. Blanket to shade out light

Now that you have your equipment I will go over some good places to star gaze for each region of the US:

North South Midwest West
Breezy Hill Vermont Big Pine Key Cherry Springs State Park Denali National Park
